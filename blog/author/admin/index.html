Boozy Blood Orange Semi-Freddo

February 12, 2017 admin Leave a comment

We used to buy this type of dessert in the super market, it tastes great but always quite pricey. It’s usually the sort of ‘top shelf’ freezer dessert costing about £4-£5 and so a bit of a treat. We never thought it could be so easy to make it your self (and a bit cheaper too!)

We’ve used blood orange juice mixed with orange liqueur in the filling to give it a citrus kick, but you can get creative here and add whatever you might happen to have in your cupboard that needs using up. I think the next one we do we might try a lemon version instead, with some limoncello and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.

This dessert is an easy one to put together and sure to impress if you’re having people over for dinner or having a romantic night in!
